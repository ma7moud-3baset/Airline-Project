About Us






Dubai International Airport is an international airport located in Dubai,
the second largest city in the United Arab Emirates,
and it is the center of Emirates Airline operations, about 2 km from the center of Dubai,
and within a few years Dubai International Airport has transformed from
just an airstrip on the shores of the Arabian Gulf,
to the most important air center in the Middle East and one of the top ten airports in the world and the fastest growing,
It currently attracts more than 115 airlines covering more than 135 destinations worldwide,
and the airport has a capacity of around 90 million passengers per year.
Dubai International Airport is one of the world's most popular airports for international passengers.
